A Los Viñateros Bravos Granítico - Cinsault 2021

Leonardo Erazo, Itata Chile

Organic, Biodynamic, Natural


Itata is a wine region located in the southern part of Chile, known for its unique terroir and the cultivation of traditional grape varieties. Itata is situated within the larger Itata Valley, which encompasses both the Itata and Ñuble provinces.


Itata has become a hub for winemakers who embrace natural and minimal-intervention winemaking practices. There is a growing movement to produce wines that express the true identity of the grapes and the terroir, with an emphasis on sustainability and authenticity. Los Vinateros Bravos is known for its commitment to natural winemaking practices. The 2021 Cinsault is a testament to this philosophy, as the winemakers prioritize minimal intervention, allowing the grapes to shine in their purest form. The result is a wine that captures the essence of the vineyard and the hands that nurtured it, creating a connection between the drinker and the land.


This wine is a testament to the reputation of Chilean winemaking and the unique character of the Cinsault grape.
