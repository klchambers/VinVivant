VinVivant Blog | Tiago Teles Vinho Branco "Raiz"

Tiago Teles Vinho Branco "Raiz", Loureiro from Barraida

Tiago Teles, Barraida Portugal

Organic


Tiago Teles Vinho Branco 'Raiz' is a notable addition to the portfolio of Portuguese winemaker Tiago Teles, known for his expertise in the Bairrada region. This skin-contact white wine reflects the indigenous grape of Loureiro and a commitment to showcasing the distinct terroir of Bairrada.


The name 'Raiz,' meaning 'Roots,' underscores the wine's connection to Bairrada's terroir. Teles sources grapes from select vineyards with diverse soils and maritime influences.


Vinho Branco 'Raiz' undergoes a meticulous winemaking process. The grapes are hand-harvested and undergo gentle pressing and 11 months of fermentation on the Lees.


On the palate, Vinho Branco 'Raiz' offers a crisp acidity with floral notes, citrus hints, and a subtle minerality.
